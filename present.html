San Serif

The Modern Standard

San Serif typefaces are widely used in web text display as well as headline display. San Serif typefaces with hairline strokes work well with the idea of minimalism, while those of bolder strokes work well in emphasizing information.

Retro

Throw back in time

Retro typefaces have grungy and rusty texture. It gives a vintage feeling and brings us back to the Industrial Revolution poster style. It is widely used in website hero image display and typographic posters.

Handwritten

Free souls

Handwritten typefaces give an organic feel. Some look more girly and some look rougher. They are also largely used in complementing hero image.
